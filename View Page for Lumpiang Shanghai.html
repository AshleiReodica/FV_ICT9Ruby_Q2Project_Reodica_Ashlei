Lumpiang Shanghai

Ingredients:

  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Garlic Powder
  • Ground black pepper
  • Parsley
  • Salt
  • Sesame oil
  • Lumpia Wrapper
  • Eggs
  • Cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. In a big bowl, combine all the vegetables, and powders, ang wet ingredients, then mix until well blended.
  2. After mixing, wrap it with lumpia wrapper, and roll it.
  3. After wrapping you can fry it until golden brown, make sure its really cooked in the inside.
  4. Lastly, add some butter on a cast iron platter, add the Sisig, then put a hot boiling sunnyside up egg while hot.
