Tapsilog

Ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Garlic
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Rice
  • Eggs

Instructions:

  1. Put all the dry ingredients in a bowl and mix it all. Add the beef to marinade and coat it well.
  2. For the Rice, put it in a pan and mix it with some Garlic to make Sinangag.
  3. For the Eggs, it can be Boiled, Scramble, or Sunnyside up. ( Depends to you )
  4. To finish, after you mixed all the ingredients and coat it to the Beef, you now have some yummy Tapa.
  5. Put the Tapa, Rice, and Eggs in one plate and now you have a delicious breakfast!
