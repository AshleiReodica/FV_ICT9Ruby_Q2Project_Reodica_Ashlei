Sisig

Ingredients:

  • Calamansi
  • Eggs
  • Chicharon
  • Pork
  • Garlic
  • Bay Leaf
  • Onion
  • Black Pepper
  • Green chili
  • Red chili
  • Vinegar
  • oil
  • mayonnaise
  • Pig Ear

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, put some water and add some pork, pigs ear, vinegar, soy sauce.Fill it all with water until its covered.
  2. Add some bay leaves, salt, pepper, and boil until the meat is no longer redish.
  3. Chop the pork and ears in 1/2inches. Mix it all in a bowl and add chili, calamansi, vinegar,salt,pepper,garlic.
  4. In a seperate pan, add pork until its hot, then add mayonnaise then combine.
  5. Lastly, add some butter on a cast iron platter, add the Sisig, then put a hot boiling sunnyside up egg while hot.
