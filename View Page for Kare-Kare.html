Kare-Kare

Ingredients:

  • Pechay
  • Bok Choy
  • Eggplants
  • Shrimp paste
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Peanuts

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, put some water and wait for it to boil.
  2. After boiling the water, add some onions and wait until tender.
  3. After that, boil the Meat until tender, then add the some crashed peanuts, or peanut butter, then wait for 5-10mins.
  4. On a seperate pan, add the garlic, eggplant, pechay, bok choy, then cook for 5mins.
  5. Transfer the cooked vegatables to a large pot, then add salt and pepper.
  6. After all this you can not enjoy some Kare-Kare and add some shrimp paste.
