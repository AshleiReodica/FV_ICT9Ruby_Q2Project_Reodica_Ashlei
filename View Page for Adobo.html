Adobo

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Soy Sauce
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Dried Bay leaves
  • Vinegar
  • Water
  • salt

Instructions:

  1. First combine the pork, soy sauce, garlic, then marinate for 1hr.
  2. In a seperate pot, put the marinated pork, then cook for few minutes.
  3. After that, add water, black peppercorn, dried leaves, then boil for 1hr.
  4. Add vinegar then waig for 10-15mins. After that add some salt.
  5. Now you have a Pork Adobo!
