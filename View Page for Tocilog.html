Tocilog

Ingredients:

  • Tocino
  • Garlic
  • Rice
  • Tomato

Instructions:

  1. For the rice, put the rice in a pan and cook it with the Garlic.
  2. After that put aside, then cook the Tocino.
  3. After all this, put it in a pan and top it with some Eggs, and not you have a Tocilog!
