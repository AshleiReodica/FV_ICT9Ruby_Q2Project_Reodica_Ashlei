Pancit Bihon

Ingredients:

  • Pancit Bihon Noodles
  • Pork
  • Chicken
  • Carrot
  • Snow Peas
  • Small chopped Cabbage
  • 1 Cup Celery
  • Copped Onion
  • 1/2 garlic minced
  • 3-4 cups of water
  • 3 tbsp of oil
  • Soy Sauce

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Pacit Bihon in a soaking water. Heat the pan with some oil, and add in the vegetables.
  2. While waiting, chop the pork and chicken, and cook, wait until they're browned and juicy.
  3. Put the water and Chicken powder at the same bowl then mix, wait until 5-10mins until it all mix together.
  4. In the pan add the vegetables, and wait until tender ( don't overcook ).
  5. Next, stir the soy sauce properly, but first remove the Pancit Bihon from the water. Give the Pancit Bihon and soy sauce a good mix.
  6. Mix the vegetables, and Pancit Bihon all together, then put it in a platem then serve it.
  7. Serve time! Enjoy your meal!
